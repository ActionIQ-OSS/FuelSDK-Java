Detailed Description

An ETTriggeredEmail represents a Triggered Email in the Salesforce Marketing Cloud. Triggered Emails are created in the Marketing Cloud Email app under the Interactions menu:

<screenshot>

Terminology note: Triggered Emails are sometimes referred to as "Triggered Sends" in the Email app, and the SOAP API refers to Triggered Emails as "Triggered Send Definitions".
